手指崩坏修复
Area: [Draw over distorted fingers] Fix: Correct human hand with 5 natural fingers, matching skin tone and lighting, same art style as the rest of the image, clean linework, anime game character

适用：修复 AI 生成角色图的手指异常，保持肤色、光影和整体画风完全一致

角色换装 / 换部件
Area: [Draw over character's clothing] Change to: The same character wearing ornate golden knight armor with red cape, elaborate pauldrons and gauntlets, fantasy RPG style, matching the character's face and pose

适用：在同一角色造型基础上更换服装或装备，保持面部、姿态完全不变

移除水印 / 多余元素
Area: [Draw over watermark text and logo] Replace with: Natural background texture matching the surrounding area, seamless texture fill, no visible traces of removed content

适用：移除水印、文字或不需要的背景元素，AI 自动生成与周围自然衔接的内容

面部细节调整
Area: [Draw over character's face area] Adjust: Same face with slightly larger eyes, softer smile, more detailed eye highlights, maintain the character's original identity and expressions

适用：微调角色面部表情和大眼效果，适合二游角色的精细化调整
